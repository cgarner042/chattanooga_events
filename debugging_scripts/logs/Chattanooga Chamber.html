Annual Meeting 2024

Signature Events

Join us for our highly anticipated Annual Meeting—the first time back in-person in 5 years—proudly presented by Chambliss, Bahner & Stophel, P.C. and EPB.

This is a special opportunity to connect with our vibrant business community and celebrate our collective achievements. We'll hit the highlights of the Chattanooga Chamber's 2023-24 accomplishments, and we'll look ahead to what the next year holds as Paul Leath, Chattanooga Gas, passes responsibility of Chamber Board Chair to Jay Dale, First Horizon Bank.

We'll also hear from Chattanooga Chamber President & CEO, Charles Wood, and recognize outstanding Chamber volunteers by presenting our Council of the Year Award and Ambassador of the Year recognition.

For faster registration and member pricing, please utilize the Member Portal by following this link: Member Portal

If you have any issues logging into your Member Portal please contact Lauren Topping 423-763-4383 or ltopping@chattanoogachamber.com.

If your organization is not currently a member of the Chattanooga Area Chamber of Commerce please register on this event page.

College and Career Fair- 2024

General

 

 

Chattanooga Area Schools College & Career Fair 

 

Sponsored by: Chattanooga Area Chamber of Commerce  

          Lookout Counseling Association

                     Chattanooga State Community College

 

The College and Career Fair will take place in the Gymnasium 

 

When: Thursday, September 5th 2024 from 9:00am - 3:00pm

            Friday, September 6th 2024 from 9:00am - 2:00pm


Where: Chattanooga State Community College

             4501 Amnicola Hwy, Chattanooga TN, 37406

 

Parking: Parking will be within walking distance of the Gym Building. 

 

Cost:  $300 for a Vendor booth: includes table, two chairs, light breakfast and lunch for two each day. 

The registration fee will be used to provide bus transportation for students to Chattanooga State Community College. 

 

In addition to joining us for this event, companies can also get involved through one of the three sponsorship levels. 

Platinum Sponsorships:

  • Vendor Booth includes table, two chairs, light breakfast and lunch for two each day. 
  • Sponsor recognition at the event
  • Sponsor recognition in advertising for event
  • Recognized in Chamber's Bottom Lines
  • Receive high visibility booth location at event noting sponsorship
  • Sponsor logo included in all marketing materials
  • Sponsor interview and company highlights marketing video

Gold Sponsorships:

  • Vendor Booth includes table, two chairs, light breakfast and lunch for two each day. 
  • Sponsor recognition at event
  • Sponsor recognition in advertising for event
  • Recognized in Chamber's Bottom Lines
  • Receive high visibility booth location at event noting sponsorship

Silver Sponsorships:

  • Vendor Booth includes table, two chairs, light breakfast and lunch for two each day. 
  • Sponsor recognition at event
  • Sponsor recognition in advertising for event
